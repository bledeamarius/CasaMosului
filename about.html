Unde ne gasesti?
Localizata in inima orasului Ocna Sugatag, our renovated house is a labor of love, meticulously crafted to offer a genuine experience of rural life in Maramureș. Every detail reflects our commitment to authenticity, from the hand-carved wooden furnishings to the intricate traditional motifs adorning the walls.
